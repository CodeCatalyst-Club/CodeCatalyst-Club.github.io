    About Code Catalyst Club

    Summer Challange is the flagship annual 24-hour Code Catalyst Club organized by KARMAVEER BHAURAO PATIL College The Summer Code Challenge is not just another coding event, it's an experience unlike any other. Organized by a team of passionate students, the Summer Code Challenge is designed to be a hybrid mode event that encourages innovation and sustainability. But that's not all! The Summer Code Challenge is also a platform for learning and growth. Participants will have access to a series of workshops and fun activities that are designed to help them develop their coding skills and network with other like-minded individuals.
    At the end of the event, participants will not only have developed innovative solutions to real-world problems, but they will also have formed lasting connections with other coders and mentors. Join us for the Summer Code Challenge and experience the thrill of coding, the joy of innovation, and the satisfaction of making a positive impact on the world.

    Schedule

    "This is where you'll find all the important details about the timeline of CodeCatalyst. Our event is designed to be a fun and engaging experience, with a mix of coding, learning, and competition. This schedule has been carefully crafted to provide a seamless and enjoyable experience for all participants.“

    > Round 1

    Quiz competition

    29th March, 2023

    The first round will be a programming quiz consisting of multiple-choice and short answer questions. The quiz will test the participants' knowledge of programming languages, concepts, and theory. The participants with the highest scores will advance the next round.

    > Round 2

    C Coding Cometition

    29th March, 2023

    The second round will be a coding competition in which participants will be given a question paper consisting of two coding problems. The participants will have a fixed amount of time to complete their solutions. The solutions will be evaluated based on accuracy, efficiency, and readability. The participants who perform the best will be selected for the final round.

    > Round 3

    Final Round

    29th March, 2023

    The final round will be an on-spot coding challenge. Participants will be given a randomly assigned programming language and a coding problem. They will have a fixed amount of time to complete their solution. The solutions will be evaluated based on accuracy, efficiency, and hardcore coding. The participants who perform the best will be declared the winners of the Summer Code Challenge.
