Event schedule ?

Round 1

Programming Quiz: The first round will be a programming quiz consisting of multiple-choice and short answer questions. The quiz will test the participants' knowledge of programming languages, concepts, and theory. The participants with the highest scores will advanceto the next round.

Round 2

Coding Competition: The second round will be a coding competition in which participants will be given a question paper consisting of two coding problems. The participants will have a fixed amount of time to complete their solutions. The solutions will be evaluated based on accuracy, efficiency, and readability. The participants who perform the best will be selected for the final round.

Round 3

On-Spot Coding Challenge: The final round will be an on-spot coding challenge. Participants will be given a randomly assigned programming language and a coding problem. They will have a fixed amount of time to complete their solution. The solutions will be evaluated based on accuracy, efficiency, and hardcore coding. The participants who perform the best will be declared the winners of the Summer Code Challenge.

Round 4

Team Challenge: If time permits, an optional team challenge may be added to the event. Participants will be placed into teams and given a more complex coding problem to solve within a fixed amount of time. The teams will be evaluated based on teamwork, solution accuracy, efficiency, and hardcore coding. The winning team will be announced at the end of the challenge.
